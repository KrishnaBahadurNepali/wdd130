vision

In order to achieve our vision, our mission is to treat every day as if it was the one and only day our guests will have to experience river side Rafting and learn geographics and to ensure they return with a better understanding and appreciation of adventure in Sunshine thopalkhola rafting.

History

Rafting in the thopalkhola/trisuli was first practiced by the local people krishna Nepali. It is a classic and luxurious raft in Nepal. Trishuli/Thopalkhola is the most popular rafting destination. A smooth and silent flowing river along with impressive gorges, exciting rapids, with a few effortless sections in between.
